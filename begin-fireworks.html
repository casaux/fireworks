Fireworks notification

Back

Persons or organisations who wish to conduct firework activities should notifty CASA at least 2 working days prior to the conduct of such activities in the following circumstances:

  • at a place within 5 nautical miles of an aerodrome (including an airfield or helipad). You must notify CASA if your firework activities are within 3 nautical miles, but we recommend notifying CASA within 5 nautical miles so we can ensure the appropriate airspace awareness to any air operations

  • if using projectiles capable of reaching more than 400 feet above ground level
